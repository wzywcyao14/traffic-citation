Welcome to the Los Angeles Parking Citation Analytics Website!

This website utilizes the dataset hosted by the city of Los Angeles to help visualize and analyze different metrics and trends about parking citations in Los Angeles.


To view raw data, please visit https://www.kaggle.com/cityofLA/los-angeles-parking-citations/version/9.
